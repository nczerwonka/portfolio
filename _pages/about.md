Game designer, writer, and researcher with experience in both academic and professional settings and a background in history and journalism. Current master's student at the University of Alberta studying digital humanities with a focus on interactive storytelling and critical media studies. Strong skills in critical media analysis and narrative design with programming skills for digital settings, as well as interpersonal development from years in customer service. 

My research interests centre on how communal storytelling and fiction intersects with, and affects, political thought and social change.  

Passionate about storytelling in the digital world!
