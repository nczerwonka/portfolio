The Martlet's feature article for their July 2025 issue, this article chronicles how guerrilla gardeners - environmental activists who shine a light on food security issues
by creating public gardens on private land - have remained an important part of Victoria's activist community in the fifteen years since the Food Not Lawn protests made national
headlines in 2010.
